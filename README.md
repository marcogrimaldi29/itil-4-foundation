## 🔑 Why ITIL 4 Foundation Matters

ITIL 4 Foundation is the **entry-level qualification** in the ITIL 4 certification scheme. It demonstrates understanding of the common language, key concepts, and how organisations can improve their work using the ITIL 4 guidance.

It is a prerequisite for all higher ITIL 4 qualifications:

- **ITIL 4 Managing Professional** (4 modules) — for practitioners managing IT-enabled services
- **ITIL 4 Strategic Leader** (2 modules) — for those involved in digital strategy
- **ITIL 4 Master** — the highest designation, requiring both MP and SL
